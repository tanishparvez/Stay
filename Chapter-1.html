About KrishnaChura

Nestled in the cultural heart of Santiniketan, KrishnaChura is a lovingly restored Bengali-style villa that offers an authentic experience of rural Bengal with all modern comforts. Named after the vibrant Krishnachura (Gulmohar) trees that dot the property, this retreat is a celebration of Bengali heritage, art, and hospitality.

The architecture reflects traditional Bengali design elements with red oxide floors, terracotta tiles, and handcrafted wooden furniture. Each corner of KrishnaChura tells a story – from the carefully curated local artwork to the handloom textiles that adorn the spaces.

Surrounded by 2 acres of lush gardens with indigenous plants, fruit trees, and flowering shrubs, KrishnaChura provides a serene escape where you can reconnect with nature while immersing yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of Rabindranath Tagore's Santiniketan.

Location & Surroundings

About the Area

KrishnaChura is nestled in the cultural heart of Santiniketan, just 3 kilometers from Visva-Bharati University established by Rabindranath Tagore. The property offers a perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility, surrounded by rural Bengal's natural beauty while remaining close to key attractions.

Distances from Key Locations:

  • Visva-Bharati University: 3 km (10 min drive)
  • Sonajhuri Haat (Craft Market): 2.5 km (8 min drive)
  • Bolpur Railway Station: 7 km (20 min drive)
  • Kolkata Airport: 180 km (3.5 hours drive)

Nearby Attractions:

Tagore's Ashram
