Cancellation Policy

  • The booking is non-transferable.
  • Cancellations made more than 25 days prior to arrival incur a 35% cancellation fee of the total tariff amount.
  • Cancellations made less than 25 days but more than 15 days prior to arrival incur a 50% cancellation fee of the total tariff amount.
  • Cancellations made less than 15 days prior to arrival or no-shows at KrishnaChura Homestay are non-refunded.
  • Bank fees, if applicable, are deducted from the refundable amount and borne by the client.
  • Refunds are processed to the client’s bank account within 14 working days from the proposed check-in date.
