Booking Policies

1.1 Reservation Process

Reservations at Stay Stories properties can be made through our official website, authorized travel partners, or by contacting our reservation team directly. All reservations are subject to availability and confirmation.

Upon confirmation of your reservation, you will receive a booking confirmation via email containing all relevant details of your stay. Please review this information carefully and contact us immediately if any corrections are needed.

1.2 Age Requirements

Guests making a reservation must be at least 18 years of age. Stay Stories reserves the right to request proof of age upon check-in.

1.3 Guest Information

When making a reservation, guests are required to provide accurate and complete information, including:

  • Full name as it appears on government-issued identification
  • Valid contact information (phone number and email address)
  • Arrival and departure dates
  • Number of guests
  • Special requests or requirements

1.4 Check-in and Check-out Times

Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM, and check-out time is 11:00 AM local time. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Guests are required to inform the property in advance if they anticipate arriving outside of standard check-in hours.

1.5 Maximum Occupancy

Each Stay Stories property has a maximum occupancy limit for safety and comfort reasons. This limit is strictly enforced and varies by property and room type. Exceeding the maximum occupancy may result in additional charges or termination of your stay without refund.

Children under 5 years of age may stay free of charge when using existing bedding. Please inform us at the time of booking if you will be traveling with children.

Payment Terms

2.1 Deposit and Payment Schedule

To secure your reservation, a deposit of 50% of the total booking amount is required at the time of booking. The remaining balance must be paid at least 14 days prior to your arrival date.

For bookings made within 14 days of arrival, full payment is required at the time of booking.

2.2 Accepted Payment Methods

Stay Stories accepts the following payment methods:

  • Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Bank transfers
  • Online payment platforms (PayPal, Razorpay)
  • Cash (accepted only for incidental charges during your stay, not for reservation payments)

2.3 Currency

All rates are quoted and charged in Indian Rupees (INR). Conversions to other currencies are approximate and subject to exchange rate fluctuations.

2.4 Taxes and Fees

All rates are subject to applicable government taxes and service charges, which will be clearly indicated during the booking process. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Goods and Services Tax (GST)
  • Service charge
  • Tourism/bed tax where applicable

2.5 Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit may be required upon check-in to cover potential damages or incidental charges. This deposit will be refunded within 7 business days after check-out, subject to a satisfactory property inspection.

The amount of the security deposit varies by property and length of stay, and will be communicated to you prior to your arrival.

Property Usage Guidelines

4.1 Responsible Use

Guests are expected to use the property and its amenities responsibly and with respect for other guests, staff, and the local community. Excessive noise, disruptive behavior, or any activities that interfere with the comfort and enjoyment of others are prohibited.

4.2 Smoking Policy

All Stay Stories properties maintain a strict no-smoking policy inside all rooms, common areas, and enclosed spaces. Designated smoking areas may be available outdoors. Violation of this policy will result in a deep cleaning fee of INR 10,000.

4.3 Pet Policy

Pet policies vary by property. Some Stay Stories locations are pet-friendly, while others do not allow pets. Please inquire about the specific policy at your chosen property before booking. Where pets are permitted, additional fees and restrictions may apply.

4.4 Property Damage

Guests will be held financially responsible for any damage to the property, furnishings, or amenities beyond normal wear and tear. This includes stains, breakage, theft, or vandalism. The cost of repair or replacement will be charged to the guest's account or security deposit.

4.5 Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited at all Stay Stories properties:

  • Parties or events without prior written approval
  • Illegal activities of any kind
  • Use of fireworks, candles, or other open flames
  • Exceeding the maximum occupancy limit
  • Commercial photography or filming without prior permission
  • Removal of any property items, including towels, robes, or décor

Violation of these prohibitions may result in immediate termination of stay without refund and potential legal action.
